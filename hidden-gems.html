Hidden Gems
Think you’ve seen and done it all in Singapore? Think again. From cooking classes at farming collective Bollywood Veggies to tours of Singapore’s first gin distillery, Tanglin Gin, there are plenty of hidden gems worth checking out in our very own backyard.

Hidden Gem
The desire to discover the road less travelled is one of the fastest-growing trends in travel. Tired of having to navigate crowds in popular attractions, many are opting for lesser-known, and lesser-visited experiences to make unique memories that serve as conversation starters at dinner parties. This desire is often met with another – wanderlust. And that’s why many of us associate taking the road less travelled with destinations outside of where we are. Due to COVID-19 and the inability to travel beyond our borders, we should take this opportunity to look inward for unique experiences and activities.
Despite its size, Singapore has so much more to offer on top of sprawling eco-friendly parks and world-class attractions such as Gardens by the Bay and Universal Studios Singapore. With such diversity of ethnicities, cultures, ideals, and ideas, Singapore has plenty of unique hidden gems out the corner of your eye, waiting to be discovered.
Did you know there are mangroves off Pulau Ubin you can only access and explore via kayak? Or that you could learn to make glutinous rice dumplings from scratch under the guidance of bak chang experts at the long-standing Kim Choo Kueh Chang? Singapore may be a small nation, but that doesn’t mean it’s short of cool under-the-radar experiences that remain unknown even to the most discerning Singaporean.
Here at Pelago, we bring you a series of amazing hidden gems, giving you the opportunity to see a different side of Singapore. Travelling beyond the Lion City may be tricky right now, but with these experiences we offer, we know you’ll realise you don’t need to leave the country to have a blast. Whether you’re into boozy experiences like a Singapore Sling Masterclass at Raffles Courtyard or a Cheese And Wine Pairing session By Basilico At Regent Singapore, or the finer things in life such as a Perfume Workshop Experience at Je taime Perfumery or a Tea Appreciation Workshop at Yixing Xuan Teahouse, we’ve got you covered.
